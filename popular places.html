Popular Places in India

Taj Mahal

Located in Agra, it is one of the Seven Wonders of the World, known for its stunning white marble architecture.

Qutub Minar

A UNESCO World Heritage Site in Delhi, it is the tallest brick minaret in the world, dating back to the 12th century.

Jaipur Palace

The City Palace in Jaipur, Rajasthan, is a stunning blend of Rajasthani and Mughal architecture.

Gateway of India

Located in Mumbai, this monumental arch was built to commemorate the visit of King George V and Queen Mary in 1911.
